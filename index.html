About Us

O.M.Ocreation is a premium fashion stone design company located at Abiola Way, behind Laroy Hotel. We specialize in high-quality stone dress designs and machine stone artistry, blending creativity with luxury.

Our mission is to bring elegance, beauty, and uniqueness to every fabric we touch. With years of experience and a passion for perfection, we create masterpieces that stand out for weddings, parties, and special events.

Every piece is crafted with attention to detail, using only the finest stones and materials. We believe that fashion is art, and we are here to make you the masterpiece.
